Picture this.
A universe where our digital selves can freely live, play and prosper.

Vana was born in San Francisco in 2021, with the idea that you should own your data and the value it creates.

By building an open ecosystem to own and experience your digital self, we are shifting the flow of data for a more prosperous world.

We’re real people. 

Nobody has all answers, especially not the people who tell you they do. We believe in
exploring things together with honesty, curiosity, and kindness. We 're constantly learning from the changing world and open to having our beliefs challenged. We learn from each other and get better together.

We love creating new things together.

That 's why we cherish and welcome creativity, knowing it will change us and the world
around us. Change isn 't always comfortable, but better IS always possible.

Maintaining a community of curious and diverse people is key. By combining our individuality, we generate originality. That 's why we welcome creativity, knowing it will change us and the world around us.

We’re our own agents.

Every human is utterly unique. We should have autonomy over ourselves and our future
and the ability to make informed choices about our data. At Vana, we prioritize systems of
accountability that allow us to exercise the fullest agency.
