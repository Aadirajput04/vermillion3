So, how does Vana work?

01. Connect

It starts with your “VNA”

Your VNA is your "virtual DNA ". That means all digital material, aka your data, captures some aspect of your essence and can be used to tailor technology to your unique needs. By using your VNA with Vana 's products, you will be able to unlock personalized, bespoke AI experiences that will change the way you surf the web.

Create your VNA by simply making a profile on Vana. Your data is safe, secured, and owned by you - forever.

What’s this all about?

Generative AI is allowing us to experience our digital selves in new, amazing ways: to augment our lives with new, out-of-this-world skills and sensations. Vana is building a place where our digital selves can freely live, play and prosper. We believe that allowing users to have ownership over their digital selves is the key to greater collaboration and advancement as geneartive AI becomes ubiquitous. By leveraging the rise of the user-owned internet, Vana is creating a decentralized system for everyone to own, use, and share their digital self, and to unlock its full potential.

What does artwork have to do with the “digital self”?

Right now, most of us may not even realise what GenAI is, let alone what they we can do with it and why it’s important for each of us to have agency over our digital selves. So the starting point is to raise awareness and build community be bringing generative AI to everyday people through immersive experience. The wonderful thing about Generative AI is that it’s innately creative. The potential to create is so utterly human that it takes us back to our earliest memories of play, fun, and wonder. The digital self offers us a unique opportunity to return to those creative havens. Through this exploration, Vana hopes to connect people with Generative AI – and, ultimately, with themselves.

Is the model that you use trained on copyrighted data?

Nope! While Vana may not create the foundation models at the heart of each person 's unique model, these models are made by leading big data and AI companies. The models are trained with public data and have restrictions to protect copyrighted works. And with Vana 's commitment to responsible use, you can rest assured that the images you upload are fully within your rights. Vana 's terms of service includes strict limitations to protect the rights of all users.
